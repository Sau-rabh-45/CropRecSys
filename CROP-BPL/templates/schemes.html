Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)
A crop insurance scheme launched to support sustainable production in agriculture by providing financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage.
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)
Aims to provide end-to-end solutions in irrigation supply chain, ensuring water accessibility for every farm and promoting efficient water use practices.
Soil Health Card Scheme
This scheme provides farmers with information on nutrient status of their soil and advice on appropriate dosage of fertilizers to improve productivity.
e-NAM (National Agriculture Market)
A pan-India electronic trading portal that networks the existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.
